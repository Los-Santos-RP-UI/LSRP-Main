Server Rules

To ensure a safe and immersive experience for everyone, all members must adhere to the following rules:

  • 1. Respect all players and staff—discrimination or harassment will not be tolerated.
  • 2. No meta-gaming, power-gaming, or unrealistic behavior.
  • 3. Use proper RP voice and actions; stay in character at all times.
  • 4. All illegal RP must be approved and realistic.
  • 5. No exploiting glitches or using unauthorized mods/scripts.
  • 6. Follow staff instructions and server guidelines at all times.
